Research Context — KansenKloof

These indicators directly map to the kansengelijkheid research agenda at Erasmus School of Economics. The KansenKaart / KansenKloof platform by Bas ter Weel, Eline Vlam, and Rineke Smilde documents inequality of opportunity across the Netherlands. High bijstand + low education + high long-term poverty is the core vulnerability cluster their research identifies.
